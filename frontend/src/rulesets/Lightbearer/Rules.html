Introduction

You don't have to read this! No prep work or prior experience is required to play Lightbearer and you cannot create a character early. This page contains some general information about how the game is played and how characters are created, but reach out directly if you have more specific questions.

You'll be selecting a race, a class, a weapon, and 3 abilities from your class. Your race will also give you an ability or two. If your race gives you multiple abilities, you don't have to choose between them.

Abilities

As you pick your abilities, some will have symbols on them:

= "Action"
= "Reaction"
= "Free Action"

In combat, players can use 1 "Action" ability during their own turn every round. Each player can also use 2 "Reaction" abilities during any turn each round.

Reactions can be used to interrupt enemies in the middle of their attacks, and are your best way of staying alive!

Free actions can be used at any time, and don't cost anything.
