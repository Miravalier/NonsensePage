Ability Basics

As you pick your abilities below, some will have symbols on them:

= "Action"
= "Reaction"
= "Free Action"

In Lightbearer, players can take 1 "Action" during their own turn and 2 "Reactions" during anyone's turn every round.

Reactions can be used to interrupt enemies in the middle of their attacks, and are your best way of staying alive!

Free actions can be taken at any time, and don't cost anything.
